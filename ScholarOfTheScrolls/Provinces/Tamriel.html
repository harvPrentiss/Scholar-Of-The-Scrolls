Tamriel

Tamriel is the main continent on Nirn and where the Elder Scrolls mostly take place. There are nine region in Tamriel.

  • Black Marsh
  • Cyrodiil
  • Elsweyr
  • Hammerfell
  • High Rock
  • Morrowind
  • Skyrim
  • SummerSet Isles
  • Valenwood

There are also other lands outside Tamriel that exist, but not much is known about them.

  • Akavir
  • Aldmeris
  • Atmora
  • Cathnoquey - A region of land between Tamriel and Akavir that was conquered by Uriel Septim V
  • Esroniet - A region ruled by Prince Bashomon who surrendered all the land to Uriel Septim V
  • Pyandonea
  • Roscrea
  • Thras
  • Yneslea - A region between Tamriel and Akavir that was conquered by Uriel Septim V. It later rebelled.
  • Yokuda
