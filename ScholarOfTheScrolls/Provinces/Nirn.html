Nirn

Nirn is the planet where the Elder Scrolls is set. It is part of the realm Mundus which floats in Oblivion. Nirn was created during the Dawn Era. Its creation was inspired by Lorkhan and it was designed by Magnus.

There are four continets on Nirn

  • Akavir
  • Atmora
  • Tamriel
  • Yokuda

There are two moons circling Nirn, Masser and Secunda. Any other planetary bodies in the sky are believed to be spheres of the Aedra and Daedra. The only exception is the Serpent constellation which is made of unstars.
