Aldmeris

Aldmeris is the fabled home of the elves before they came to Tamriel. There is no physical evidence that this land actually existed. Even the Elder Scrolls have no mention of the land. It is believed that the elven people combined memories of their various homelands into this one land of legend. The rumored location is to the southwest of Tamriel.
